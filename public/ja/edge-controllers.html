    Measurement & 3D ProfilingWhat the measurement layer does

    Hand calipers and sampling miss drift, deform soft parts, and leave no audit trail. GYUTRON gauges every part inline with telecentric optics and laser 3D profiling — height, gap, flatness, and form measured to sub-micron repeatability, traceable to gauge blocks, logged for SPC.

    Telecentric Dimensional GaugingEdge, diameter, gap, and concentricity to ±0.8 µm 2σ. Distortion-free telecentric optics; no contact, no part deformation.
    Laser 3D ProfilingSingle-line and snapshot profilers reconstruct height, step, flatness, and warpage. Z-repeatability to 0.5 µm at full scan speed.
    Surface Form & HeightCoplanarity, ball/lead height, weld bead, glue-line volume. Dense point clouds resolve features 2D imaging cannot reach.
    Traceable & SPC-ReadyGauge-block-referenced calibration with R&R support. Every result time-stamped to the part ID and streamed to SPC.
    Identification & TraceabilityFrom mark to traceable record

    Low-contrast DPM, damaged labels, and high-speed lines push hand scanners and fixed readers past their limits — and a single no-read breaks the genealogy chain. GYUTRON reads 1D/2D/DPM codes, OCR text, and RFID tags, then links each ID into an end-to-end track-and-trace record from raw material to shipped unit.

    1D/2D/DPM code readingData Matrix, QR, PDF417, and all common 1D codes. Dot-peen and laser DPM on metal, PCB, and curved parts decoded under glare and tilt.
    OCR / OCV text readingReads printed and engraved lot, date, and serial strings. Verifies characters against expected format; rejects malformed or missing marks.
    RFID + barcode captureUHF/HF RFID handhelds and rugged scanners for cartons, totes, and pallets. Bulk-read tagged loads where line-of-sight to a label fails.
    End-to-end genealogyEach read links part, process, operator, and parent assembly. Full forward/backward trace; scoped recall by lot, shift, or component batch.
    Warehouse & Logistics AutomationRugged capture at every touch point

    Paper picklists and after-shift batch uploads leave inventory wrong and orders short. GYUTRON puts rugged scanning at every touch — receiving, put-away, picking, shipping — so every move is confirmed at the source and the WMS stays live.

    Guided mobile workflowsRugged Android PDAs run directed put-away, pick paths, and scan-to-confirm tasks. IP65, 1.5 m drop, -20°C freezer-rated, full-shift battery.
    First-pass scan reliability1D/2D and DPM engines clear damaged, frosted, and low-contrast labels at 99.5%+ first read — long-range to 12 m for rack-high pallets.
    Bulk RFID inventoryUHF handhelds read 600+ tags/s for cycle counts, dock-door dispatch, and pallet verification — count a bay without touching a carton.
    Industrial Sensing & Condition MonitoringFrom part presence to machine health

    Presence checks that drop out in glare. Distance feedback that drifts. Cabinets that overheat with no warning. GYUTRON combines proximity, photoelectric, and laser sensing with environmental and condition monitoring, all reporting over IO-Link to your edge controller.

    Proximity & PresenceInductive, capacitive, and magnetic detection. Metal target sensing to 40 mm, flush-mount bodies, 1 ms response on conveyors and fixtures.
    Photoelectric & DistanceThrough-beam, retro, and diffuse modes. Background suppression to 5 m, stable on clear, glossy, and dark targets where standard sensors drop out.
    Laser MeasurementTriangulation displacement to ±2 µm, time-of-flight to 50 m. Gap, height, level, and runout checks at up to 8 kHz sampling.
    Environmental SensingPressure, temperature, humidity, flow, and air quality. Calibrated ranges with analog and digital output for process stability.
    Vision-Guided Robotics & Bin PickingFrom jumbled bin to robot coordinates

    Hard-coded fixtures and jigs break the moment parts arrive jumbled, mixed, or unfixtured. GYUTRON gives robots eyes: 2D/3D vision computes the 6-DoF pose of each part in a bin or on a conveyor, plans a collision-free grasp, and streams coordinates to any robot brand. Pick randomly stacked, shiny, and dark parts at production cycle times without re-tooling.

    Random Bin PickingPick fully overlapping, unfixtured parts. Per-cycle 3D capture, segmentation, and grasp ranking. No jigs, no part presentation.
    6-DoF Part LocalizationCAD-matched pose: X/Y/Z + roll/pitch/yaw. < 0.1 mm position, < 0.2 deg orientation. Output in robot base frame.
    Shiny & Dark SurfacesMulti-exposure HDR and blue-light projection recover point clouds on chrome, black plastic, and machined metal.
    Robot-Agnostic GuidanceNative drivers for FANUC, ABB, KUKA, Yaskawa, UR, and more. Collision-checked grasps, TCP path, and approach vector.
    Composite Mobile Robots & Smart WorkcellsOne mobile cell that sees, decides, picks, and drives

    Mobile manipulators that drive between stations, locate parts with 3D vision, pick to +/-0.05 mm, and read the code to confirm the right part landed in the right place. AMR base, cobot arm, vision, and code reading from one vendor.

    3D vision + code readingArea-scan and 3D heads locate parts at +/-0.05 mm; on-arm code reader confirms 1D/2D/DPM identity at 99.5% first pass.

    Cobot handling6-axis collaborative arm, force-limited for shared space; +/-0.02 mm repeatability, tool-change for grippers and probes.
    Autonomous mobilitySLAM-guided AMR base docks at +/-100 m and 1 deg; safety-rated lidar, no floor magnets or tape, IP54 chassis.
