Application detail

Mobile manipulators feeding 3C and semiconductor lines

Challenge
Reels, trays, and carriers move between SMT feeders, test handlers, and stockers by manual cart. Operators walk parts station to station, mis-loads stop the line, and there is no per-carrier record of who touched what or when.
Approach
A mobile manipulator -- AMR base, cobot arm, 3D area-scan head, and on-arm code reader -- drives to each station and docks at +/-100 m. The 3D head locates the carrier and corrects for docking offset; the arm picks to +/-0.05 mm. The on-arm reader confirms the DPM or 2D code before release, and the edge controller reports pose, identity, and AMR position to MES every cycle.
Result
Hands-free transfer between tools, <8 s per pick-and-place, 99.5% first-pass code read, and zero wrong-slot loads released. Full chain-of-custody per carrier; one fleet tends multiple lines instead of operators walking parts.
