Solutions

Robotics solutions for the places work actually happens.

GyuTron bundles hardware, configuration, and support around high-value operational workflows so customers can move from pilot to production faster.

Automated inspection

Combine smart cameras, laser sensors, and robot guidance to inspect parts, labels, seams, surfaces, and packaging without slowing the line.

Warehouse mobility

Rugged PDAs and scan workflows connect receiving, inventory, picking, dispatch, and field returns with real-time operational data.

Manufacturing cells

Robot arms, sensors, grippers, and safety controls packaged for loading, unloading, sorting, light assembly, and repetitive station work.

Electronics assembly

SCARA-style motion, vision alignment, and precision sensing support compact, fast-moving electronic component workflows.

Automotive operations

Deploy weld support, part verification, barcode traceability, and line-side material handling for modern automotive supply chains.

Medical and pharma

Clean, repeatable, traceable systems for serialization, environmental checks, packaging validation, and controlled production support.
